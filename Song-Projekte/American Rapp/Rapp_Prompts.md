
### 1. Der Klassische Street-Style (Boom Bap)

Wenn du den Fokus auf die Stimme und einen schweren Beat legen willst:

> **Style:** `90s East Coast Boom Bap, Gritty Street Rap, Hard-Hitting Drums, Dusty Samples, Heavy Bass, Aggressive Vocals, 90 BPM`

### 2. Der Moderne Aggressive-Style (Dark Trap)

Falls Umberto eher "schreien" oder sehr druckvoll rappen soll:

> **Style:** `Dark Cinematic Trap, Aggressive Distorted 808, Gritty Vocals, Eerie Synth Pads, Hard Percussion, Urban Atmosphere, 140 BPM`

### 3. Der Hybrid-Style (Crossover)

Passend zu deinem Interesse an Hard Rock/Emo-Trap-Elementen:

> **Style:** `Phonk-inspired Trap, Distorted Bass, Gritty Male Vocals, Memphis Rap Aesthetic, Dark Melodic Piano, High Energy, 130 BPM`
